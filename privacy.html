Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our app Road Motions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information through Firebase services:

  • Your name, email, and password (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Student data (name, email, progress reports), accessible only to the registered user who created them

1.1 Local Device Storage

To enhance performance and offline access, our app may store non-sensitive data locally on your device using secure storage methods (such as SharedPreferences). This includes information like the timestamp of your last data sync or interface preferences. This data remains on your device and is not shared with external servers unless part of a sync process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow access to and management of student profiles
  • Improve educational animations and app functionality

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on:

  • Your consent (given during registration)
  • Our legitimate interest to provide and improve our educational services

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, Please contact us.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore and protected by encryption and authentication protocols.

6. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Firebase – authentication and cloud data storage
  • Lottie by Airbnb – for displaying animations

7. Use of Device Features

With your permission, our app may access your device’s camera or file system if you choose to attach screenshots, photos, or documents while using features such as contact support or sending progress reports (e.g., via email). This access is optional, user-initiated, and no files are uploaded or accessed automatically without your action. You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 (or under 16 in the EU) without guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about your data, contact us at rmroadmotions@gmail.com.
