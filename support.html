Road Motions – Support

Welcome to the support page for Road Motions, a learning and progress-tracking tool built for driving instructors. Road Motions helps instructors deliver animated lessons, manage student progress, and provide timely feedback — all in one place.

Features Overview

  • View animated driving lessons
  • Add and manage students by name and email
  • Log progress reviews for each student
  • Email reviews directly to students

Frequently Asked Questions

My Account

  • Q: How do I view my account details?
    A: Tap on the Account icon, which can be seen on the top right of the app bar.
  • Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: Go to the Accounts page, then tap 'Change password' and follow the instructions shown on screen to reset your password.
  • Q: How do delete my account?
    A: Go to the Accounts page, tap the 'Delete account', and follow the instructions that pop up, to delete all account information from you account including students, and progress reports linked to the account.
  • Q: Can other instructors see my students?
    A: No. Only the instructor who added a student can view or edit that student’s progress.

Students

  • Q: How do I add a student?
    A: Go to the student section, tap '+ Add student', then enter their name and email, and add student.
  • Q: How do I edit a student?
    A: Go to the student list, tap the student's name, then tap the 'edit' button next to their name to update their details.
  • Q: How do I delete a student?
    A: Go to the student list, next to the student's name will be a 'passed' button, when you click 'passed' you will have a pop up which will alert you to allow deleting the student, and there relevant data as well as their progress reviews.

Progress & Reviews

  • Q: How do I create a progress review for a student?
    A: Click on the student you wish to create a progress review for. Then click '+ Add progress review', and score the relevant fields, and click 'View summary'. NOTE: must be saved first (by pressing save on summary view), then you can email from student view of summary.
  • Q: How do I email a progress review to a student?
    A: Tap on the student's name, review their progress log, and tap 'Send Email'. The app will produce a email in your default email app, which allows you to send the progress review to the saved student email address.

Lessons

  • Q: What are lessons based on?
    A: The lessons are designed to illustrate a driving concept visually.
  • Q: How do I view a lesson?
    A: Go to the Lessons page, and select the relevant category, and then select the lesson you wish to view, and click play.
  • Q: How to find relevant lessons within a lesson?
    A: When you are viewing a lesson, you can find relevant lessons by clicking 'Road options'.
